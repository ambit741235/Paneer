# Ingredients for Vanilla Cake
1. 2/3 cup all purpose flour
2. 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
3. 1 and 1/4 egg
4. 1/3 teaspoon baking soda
5. 1 and 1/3 tablespoon raisins
6. 1/2 cup powdered sugar
7. 2 and 3/4 tablespoon milk
8. 1/2 cup butter
9. 1/3 teaspoon vanilla essence
10. 1 and 1/3 tablespoon cashews
11. 1/3 teaspoon salt
